Invitation & Onboarding: Screen Journey

The path from clicking the invite email to landing inside the workspace, for both a brand-new user and an existing user, with forgot-password wired in. The system resolves the invite first and routes people to the right screen, so no one has to self-diagnose whether they are new or existing.

Rough, on-brand mockup: layout and copy are illustrative, not pixel-final. Design system: Untitled UI patterns, brand purple #711FFF, real Net Net logo. Non-interactive.
